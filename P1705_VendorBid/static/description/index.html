About Vendor Bid

Revolutionize Supply Chain Management

Vendor Bid is a cutting-edge Odoo 18 module designed to revolutionize supply chain management. From secure email verification and supplier registration to RFP creation, quotation management, and advanced analytics, it empowers businesses with seamless, data-driven procurement workflows.

Explore Vendor Bid Features

Comprehensive Procurement Tools

Email Verification with OTP

Securely validate vendor email addresses with OTP-based verification. Only new, non-blacklisted emails are accepted, ensuring a trusted supplier base.

  • Validates email uniqueness and blacklist status
  • Sends OTP to valid IPT emails for verification
  • Navigates to registration form upon successful OTP

Comprehensive Registration Form

A robust, five-section form for vendor onboarding, covering company details, financials, client references, certifications, and document submissions, with strict validation.

  • Company info, contacts, and validated trade/TIN numbers
  • Financial details with bank account validation
  • Dynamic client references (up to 5)
  • Non-expired certifications and document uploads (≤1MB)
  • Automatic validation with user-friendly error messages

Two-Step Verification Process

A secure approval workflow with initial review and final approval, ensuring only qualified vendors are onboarded with automated account creation.

  • Reviewers approve, reject, or blacklist with comments
  • Approvers finalize decisions, creating vendor records
  • Email notifications with login credentials for suppliers
  • Supports list, form, Kanban, and search views

RFP Creation & Quotation Submission

Streamlined RFP creation by reviewers, with a supplier portal for quotation submission, RFQ generation, and reviewer scoring.

  • Reviewers create RFPs with list and form views
  • Suppliers submit quotations via portal
  • Automatic RFQ creation and reviewer notifications
  • Scoring based on delivery, charges, and terms
  • Workflows for draft, submitted, approved, and closed states

RFP Published

After the final approve the requested products are published in the portal with number of quantity and additional information.

  • Supplier can see the Rfp
  • Suppliers submit quotations via portal
  • Automatic RFQ creation and reviewer notifications

Approval Process

Total approval process From creating RFP to publishing and supplier Biding to bid approve.

RFP

  • Reviewer creates RFP then submit it
  • Approver approved
  • RFP post in portal

After RFP post in portal All the registered supplier will get email. Then they can bid the product which are in the RFP. And then submit. A supplier can submit more than one bid. When The approver close the rfp time then RFQ approval process start.

RFQ Approval Process

  • Reviewer recommends and gives a score 1-10 to all the recommended.
  • Recommended RFQ goes to the approver
  • Approver approves one rfq. He can not approve more than one RFQ for one RFP.
  • Automatically creates a new purchase order

RFP Reporting

Generate detailed Excel and HTML reports for RFPs, filtered by date range and supplier name, for comprehensive procurement insights.

  • Excel reports with customizable filters
  • HTML previews for web-based reporting
  • Includes supplier details, RFP status, and product lines

RFP Dashboard

A dynamic dashboard with graphs and charts showcasing approved RFQs, total amounts, and product-wise breakdowns.

  • Metrics for approved RFQs and total amounts
  • Product-wise breakdown charts
  • Real-time data visualization

How to Install

Get Started with Vendor Bid

  1. Visit the Odoo Apps Store and search for "Vendor Bid".
  2. Click "Install" to add the module to your Odoo 18 instance.
  3. Configure settings via the Odoo Apps menu.
  4. Configure Outgoing Email server if not configured.
  5. Start managing vendors and RFPs effortlessly!
